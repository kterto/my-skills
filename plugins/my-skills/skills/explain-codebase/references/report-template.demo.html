Scope

billing-service · src/billing/**

Inferred purpose

A standalone billing service that turns draft invoices into settled, paid records. It computes region-aware tax, charges customers through Stripe, and keeps an append-only ledger so that finance can reconcile every cent. Subscriptions drive recurring invoices; webhooks keep payment state in sync under retries.
